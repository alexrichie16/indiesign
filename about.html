About

Indie

Indie is Indiesign's founder and current main constituent. Indie has been designing websites since he was a kid using website building services such as WordPress and Weebly. However, as he grew older he expanded into developing websites utilizing pure Hypertext Markup Language (HTML), Cascading Style Sheets (CSS), and JavaScript. He is also well-versed in Python and C++ languages.

Indie really enjoys applying mathematics when he is developing webites. One of his favorite uses is utilizing and embedding linear algebra for experience personalization features.

He has also always had an affinity for photography— some of his photos are utilized here on this very website.

Indie is able to communicate effectively in English, Español, Deutsch, Portugês and Français. So don't be afraid to reach out if you are non-english speaking.
